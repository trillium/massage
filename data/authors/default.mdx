    I've had a deep abiding love and respect for the esoteric arts for over thirt-five years. As a practicing astrologer for thirty and a tarot reader for twenty, the bliss of sharing cosmic knowledge has enriched my respect for the joy, sorrows, and creative possibilities that galvanizes everyone's lives. I've learned to appreciate the authenticity of this through the many clients whose sincere need has found solace in the Great Mystery of trusting one tuned into the unknown.
    Astrology allows both the tangible and the invisible to communicate on such subjects as who we are, how we manifest our highest potential, the importance of loved ones in our lives, the timing of specific endeavors, and what life is bringing us as we go through the round of changes that every year brings. Natal, transit, progression, relationship, and horary charts answer these questions. Tarot, through prayer and sacred images opens the link between ancestors and the Divine Mother Father God. Using these tools, we engage in a spiritual practice that joins our personality and Soul self on a daily basis if we so choose. With happiness we move lightly through the challenges that life presents. The magical, etheric, healing principles of flower essences, with affirmations, add to my work as well. The essences bring a devic level of healing to body/mind/soul issues uncovered through astrology and tarot. The physical is directly connected to the etheric through the emotional body. In this way the feeling nature aids our body's healing by using flower essences. A reading from me gives you both spiritual insight, recognition of the path with the most love, and if necessary, a spiritual intervention.
    My heart is pure and wants only what is the best for you. I have no judgement about your circumstance. I believe this life dance we are all engaged within is about Love and letting go of the bondage that separates us from Love. Your reading will confirm and renew your faith in your life's purpose. That is my gift and my promise to you.
    Blessed Be.
