# Airbnb Hosts: Get a Free 30-Minute Upgrade

If you're an Airbnb host, I want to do something nice for you.

**Book a massage with me and I'll add 30 minutes to your session for free.** That's it. No catch, no code, no expiration date. Just mention that you're an Airbnb host when you book.

## Why Airbnb Hosts?

Hosting is a lot of work. You're cleaning, coordinating, responding to messages at all hours, dealing with last-minute cancellations, and making sure every guest feels at home. It's hospitality in the truest sense, and most people don't realize how physically and mentally demanding it is.

I joined [Airbnb's Services platform](/blog/now-on-airbnb) last year and got a front-row seat to how much hosts put into their listings. You deserve some of that hospitality directed back at you.

## How It Works

1. **Book a massage** — on [Airbnb](https://airbnb.com/sv/trilliummassage), [directly through my site](/book), or however you prefer
2. **Mention you're an Airbnb host** when you book or when I arrive
3. **Enjoy the extra time** — your session gets 30 minutes longer, on the house

That's a 60-minute session becoming 90, or a 90-minute session becoming two hours. Same price, more time to work out the knots from flipping mattresses and hauling linens.

## Any Platform, Any Session

This isn't limited to booking through Airbnb. Book however works best for you — the upgrade applies regardless. I just want to make it easy.

## A Tip for Your Guests

If you're looking to level up your hosting game, in-home massage is one of the highest-rated add-ons guests can book. Feel free to share my [Airbnb listing](https://airbnb.com/sv/trilliummassage) in your guidebook or welcome message. Your guests get a great experience, and you get a better review. Win-win.
